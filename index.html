Our Services

Basic Cleaning

Everyday upkeep: dusting, mopping, beds, kitchen & bath surfaces.

All Rooms:

  • Dust & clean furniture
  • Clean light switches
  • Clean TV screens
  • Pick up & straighten
  • Make beds
  • Change linens (if left on bed)
  • Vacuum carpets
  • Sweep & mop floors
  • Empty wastebaskets

Bathrooms:

  • Disinfect toilets, tubs, showers
  • Clean sinks, counters, mirrors
  • Polish chrome
  • Sweep & mop floors
  • Change towels (if left for us)

Kitchen:

  • Sanitize sinks & polish chrome
  • Wipe counters, cabinets (exterior)
  • Clean appliance exteriors
  • Load dishwasher (upon request)
  • Clean inside microwave
  • Sweep & mop floors

Deep Cleaning

Includes Basic Cleaning + deeper scrubbing, polishing & detail work.

  • Everything in Basic Cleaning
  • Scrub baseboards & walls
  • Clean inside cabinets & drawers
  • Clean behind appliances
  • Polish furniture & fixtures
  • Spot-clean windows & doors

Office Cleaning

Reliable, professional service for workspaces and commercial areas.

  • Dust & wipe desks, shelves, surfaces
  • Disinfect restrooms
  • Vacuum carpets & mop hard floors
  • Empty trash & recycling bins
  • Clean interior glass & windows
  • Sanitize high-touch areas (handles, switches)
